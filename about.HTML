About Me

Hello visitors! and thank you for visiting my portfolio. Youre now in the "About Me" section. There are multiple aspects about my background and skills that I'd like to share with you. I have a diverse background in various fields, including web development, design, and creative writing. My journey began with a passion for technology and creativity, which led me to explore different avenues in the digital world. I can hear your eyes rolling in the background as you read this, but stick with me and I'll share some of my experiences and accomplishments with you.

Background

My love for technology and creativity has been the driving force behind my career choices and personal growth. It began where it all stats for most people... the first time I touched a computer. See, I was born and raised in Jamaica, and when you are raised in a third world country, opportunities are limited but not impossible. fro the age of 1-10 the closest i came to a computer was the NES and a barely functional one at that. At the tender age of 11 my mother (give it up for my momma!) was able to bring both myself and my sister to America... where were were prompty robbed and stranded in New York. while it was a harsh reality check my Mother never let it show how much this affected her. instead she took the hand she was dealt and with her two kids in tow she made the best of it. Cut to 2 years later in Georgia, where a close friend of mine (the fisrt friend I made in that state) introduced me to videogames via City of heroes/ City of villians. I was instantly hooked and wanted more videogames. I couldnt get enough of them. From pc to hand helds I was obsessed. Videogames and computers, in conjunction, were my life for the next few years. I went through middle school and then highschool lookign for ways to get into the hobby. Unfortunately there wernt a lot of resources available to me back then. I didnt let that deter me though. I dismantled and reassembled varoius electronincs around the home, to my mothers dismay, and steadly fed my curiosity. Another cut to the end of highschool and I needed a way to fund my college education. Thus I turned to the military. This is where my love for tech really took a turn for the better. I had my own money and my own space to experiment. I spen an exorbitant amount of money on computer parts. I learned how they fit together and how they communicated. Unfortunately at that time i was not in a software role in the military, no I was a Mechanic. It wasnt until I left the military where iw as able to persue my love for tech into the IT space. after a few jobs I was introduced to web dev and networking. those 2 were polar opposites for me, and i quickly knew what side of the spectrum i wanted to be on. Ive since poured all my education benefits into becoming a front/back end developer and now am aiming to be an AI development engineer. Im not a one trick pony though. I have a love for multiple forms of expession. during my adult years I have been able to persue various creative outlets. those love include Creative writing, sewing & designing, and inventing. Ill do my best to share some of my work in those areas as well. I hope you enjoy your time here and feel free to reach out.
